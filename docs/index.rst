Developing Fundamental AI Programming Skills for Drug Discovery
===============================================================

This guide contains the content for **Jupyter notebooks and portal access at TACC**
and **Python fundamentals and foundations for AI**. The objectives of these
sections are to introduce the use of Jupyter notebooks at TACC, and to
introduce the most fundamental Python skills that are necessary
for domain scientists to be successful in AI coding projects.
